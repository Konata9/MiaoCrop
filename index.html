MiaoCrop - Secure Client-side Image Cropper

MiaoCrop is a free, privacy-focused image tool that runs entirely in your browser. No images are uploaded to any server.

Key Features

  • Secure & Private: 100% client-side processing. Your data never leaves your device.
  • Image Cropping: Crop, rotate, and flip images with ease.
  • Format Conversion: Convert images to PNG, JPEG, and WebP.
  • Icon Generator: Create icon packs for various platforms instantly.
  • Background Removal: Remove white backgrounds to create transparent PNGs.

How to Use

  1. Upload an image by dragging and dropping or selecting a file.
  2. Use the editor to crop, rotate, or flip your image.
  3. Select your desired export format (Standard, Icons, or Covers).
  4. Download your processed image instantly.
